About Rebru

From Residue to Ritual

At Rebru, we specialize in transforming spent coffee grounds into high-value climate products and regenerative materials. Based in Makassar, South Sulawesi, we are one of the first startups in the region addressing the rapidly growing challenge of coffee waste.

Our core expertise lies in collecting, processing, and upgrading spent coffee grounds into impactful products such as biochar, compost, and bio-briquettes.

The Core Problem

Why Coffee Waste Is Our Focus?

01

High Volume

Coffee consumption in Indonesia is the highest in Southeast Asia, creating consistent, predictable waste volumes.

02

Hidden Emissions

Spent coffee grounds are an underestimated methane emitter when left to decompose in landfills.

03

High Potential

High-quality biochar and clean energy can be produced from SCG (Spent Coffee Grounds) when processed correctly.
