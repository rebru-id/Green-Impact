About Us

Every year, millions of tons of coffee grounds end up in landfills. When left to decompose alongside other organic waste, these grounds release methane gas—one of the most harmful greenhouse gases driving climate change. In a city like Makassar, where coffee culture is rapidly growing, this challenge becomes even more visible: from cafés to households, coffee waste keeps piling up every single day.

For us, coffee waste is not the end of the story. It is the beginning of a new journey. What many see as residue, we see as an opportunity to give a second life. We believe that what is often overlooked can be transformed into something useful, valuable, and even beautiful when handled with care.

Behind every product we create—from soap, candles, and car fragrances to biochar—lies a simple conviction: nothing should go to waste. Every grain of coffee grounds carries a story, and through the right hands, that story can be rewritten into a solution for our planet.

Rebru stands as a reminder to all of us: from the small remnants left in your coffee cup, we can spark big changes that protect the environment, empower communities, and build a greener future.

And now, we invite you to be part of this journey. Whether you are a coffee shop, a business, or an individual who cares about the planet, your involvement matters. Together, we can ensure that no coffee waste goes to waste—and that every cup contributes to a more sustainable tomorrow.
